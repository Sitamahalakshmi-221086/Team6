Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about CampusPlace.

How do I register as a student?
Click "Join Now" and select the Student role. Fill in your college email, student ID, and branch details. Once verified by your TPO, you'll get full access to job listings and placement drives.
Can companies post jobs directly?
Yes! Verified recruiters can post job openings, schedule campus drives, and directly shortlist students. Register as a Company/Recruiter to get started.
What if I forgot my password?
On the Login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
How long until I get a response?
Our team typically responds within 24 hours on business days (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–5 PM). For urgent matters, call or email the relevant department directly.
Is CampusPlace available for all colleges?
Currently CampusPlace is built for RGUKT campuses in Andhra Pradesh. We're actively expanding. Reach out to our partnerships team to bring CampusPlace to your college.
