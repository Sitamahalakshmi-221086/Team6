# CampusPlace – Campus Placement Management Portal

> **"Your Campus. Your Career. One Smart Platform."**

CampusPlace is a centralized, modern placement management portal designed to streamline the recruitment process. It connects university students, companies, and the Training and Placement Office (TPO) with smart tools, real-time tracking, and a seamless digital experience.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🎓 For Students
- **Smart Profiles**: A professional presence showcasing academic details, skills, and resumes.
- **Skill-Based Matching**: Automatically get job recommendations that fit your branch, CGPA, and expertise.
- **One-Click Apply**: Apply to roles instantly and track your status in real-time.
- **Modern Dashboards**: A premium interface to manage applications, download resources, and prepare for interviews.

### 🏢 For Companies
- **Effortless Job Postings**: Post detailed job descriptions with specific eligibility criteria.
- **Advanced Filtering**: Filter and shortlist students based on skills, CGPA, and branch.
- **Hybrid Testing Mode**: Support for both online (e.g., HackerRank) and offline exam coordination.
- **Streamlined Workflow**: Manage candidates from application through to final selection.

### 🛡️ For TPO / Admin
- **Full Placement Oversight**: Monitor all campus drives, application trends, and placement rates.
- **Corporate Relations**: Manage and approve company registrations and job postings.
- **Data-Driven Insights**: Generate detailed placement reports and statistics.
- **Secure Access**: Role-based authentication to ensure data privacy and integrity.

## 🎨 Design & Aesthetics

CampusPlace is built with a **Premium, Modern UI** focus:
- **Responsive Interface**: Works flawlessly on desktops, tablets, and phones.
- **Standardized Design**: Cohesive profile banners and layouts across all user roles.
- **Professional Iconography**: Powered by **Lucide Icons** for a crisp, modern look.
- **Elegant Typography**: Utilizing **Syne** and **DM Sans** for maximum readability and style.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript
- **Styling**: CSS3 (Modular & Standardized)
- **Icons**: [Lucide Icons](https://lucide.dev/)
- **Typography**: [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/) (Syne, DM Sans, Plus Jakarta Sans)

---

## 📂 Project Structure

- `/Frontend/index.html`: Main landing page for the portal.
- `/Frontend/pages/`: Role-specific dashboards and authentication pages.
- `/Frontend/css/`: Centralized and modular style sheets.
- `/Frontend/js/`: Client-side logic and interactivity.
